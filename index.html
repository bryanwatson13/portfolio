About

Design that lives beyond the screen

I'm Bryan Watson, a UX/UI Product Designer and Researcher who approaches digital products through a systems lens. My work spans interaction design, user research, and service design — always in service of the person on the other side of the screen.

I've worked on products at scale, including Amazon Buy with Prime, where a checkout feature turned into a full service design challenge touching logistics, physical infrastructure, and partner ecosystems.

Let's Talk →
My Process

I use the Double Diamond — with a service design lens at every stage.

01

Discover

Competitive audits, social listening, stakeholder mapping — understanding users and the systems around them.

02

Define

Journey maps, service blueprints, user flows — framing the problem across all actors and touchpoints.

03

Develop

Sketches, lo-fi wireframes, iterative testing — exploring solutions with real users at every stage.

04

Deliver

High-fidelity prototypes in Figma, dev-ready handoff, and documented design decisions.
